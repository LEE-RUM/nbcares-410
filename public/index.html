What Is NB CARES?

NB CARES is a unique self-sufficiency program serving New Britain residents. Multiple agencies will collaborate with city residents to increase economic self-sufficiency and build social capital. We are committed to delivering holistic services that helps your family’s bottom line. We developed a Passport for Success that allows you to track your progress. Also, since you’ve told us it’s hard to stay “in the know” about programming that helps your family, we built a One-Stop-Shop community calendar and resource menu that will be maintained by ParaDYM Academy, Inc.
