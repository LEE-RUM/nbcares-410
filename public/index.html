What Is NBCares?

NBCares is unique in New Britain. Care coordinators from multiple agencies will collaborate with city residents on increasing economic self-sufficiency AND building a professional network. We are committed to delivering holistic services that helps your family’s bottom line. We have developed a Passport for Success that helps everyone in the network keep track of your progress and demonstrates progress. Finally, we have noticed that it’s difficult to stay “in the know” about programming that helps families so we will become a One-Stop Shop for citywide programming in collaboration with ParaDYM Academy, Inc.
