The Housing Authority of the City of New Britain is a public agency located in New Britain, a city of 70,000 people situated on approximately 13.1 square miles in central Connecticut.

The Housing Authority manages 804 low-income housing units at seven developments throughout the city, half of which house the elderly and/or disabled with the remainder reserved for use by families.

In addition, the Housing Authority administers Section 8 Tenant Based Assistance Vouchers which provide rental assistance to 851 families residing in the private market in New Britain.

Our Vision

New Britain residents will achieve positive life outcomes by increasing social and personal capital


Mission

A network of agencies delivering coordinated services to people working towards self-sufficiency
