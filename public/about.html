About Us

The NB CARES Program – or Collaborating Actively on Resident Empowerment and Success in New Britain kicked off as a pilot in 2020. Additional partners have been added to allow city residents a “Choose Your Own Adventure” that results in increasing or achieving self-sufficiency – which is a decreased reliance on federal and state aid.

Our Vision Statement is simple: New Britain residents will achieve positive life outcomes by increasing social and personal capita
